About our work

Studio and Design is a solo entrepreneur design firm based out of Austin Tx that utilize's a host of web development platforms including Wordpress, Wix, Bootstrap and other custom designs to create cutting edge sites for the home and start up business. We offer a multitude of options with our sites which cover continual maintenance, site updates, and user support.

Projects and Designs

Our Goal

Studio Designs goal is to establish a continued working relationship with our clients to satisfy the needs of any small business website. This begins from creating a unique site which is tailored to your needs, all the way through continual maintenance and updates on a per term basis.
Our product line has a number of options to choose from, starting with a single site project, to multi-level updates of content, media and social networking. No matter what your needs may be, each of the subscriptions and site plans will be custom tailored to fit any small business goals.
